Cutting Patient Admin Overhead at a Multi-Site Private Healthcare Group

Private GP & Physio Group 3 clinics — 52 staff London & Home Counties Challenge: Operational admin, not clinical AI

The Situation

A growing private GP and physiotherapy group was drowning in appointment management. Three receptionists across two sites were spending more than half their working day on inbound calls — booking, rescheduling, chasing referral letters, and following up on no-shows.

Referral letter drafting was taking 3–4 days. A new site was opening in six months, and they knew they couldn't hire their way out of the problem.

They had no AI in place. Their automation score was low. They didn't know where to start.

Assessment finding: 67% of receptionist time was spent on tasks that follow a predictable, rule-bound pattern — ideal candidates for AI automation with zero clinical risk.

What We Built

QuietGate
Deployed as the first point of contact for all inbound appointment enquiries — via phone callback request, email, and web form. Classified patient intent, checked availability, and routed complex cases to staff. Non-clinical triage handled entirely without human involvement.
QuietOps
Automated the full appointment confirmation, reminder, and rescheduling workflow. Triggered 48-hour and 2-hour SMS reminders with one-tap rescheduling links, eliminating the manual chase cycle that consumed receptionist time daily.
QuietDraft (custom skill)
Automated referral letter generation — pulling relevant patient notes, applying the GP's preferred formatting template, and producing a draft ready for clinical sign-off in under 4 minutes. Letters still require a clinician to review and approve before sending.

All built to NHS IG standards. GDPR-compliant data handling. Integrated with their existing practice management system. No changes to clinical workflows.
