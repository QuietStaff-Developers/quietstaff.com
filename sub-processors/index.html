What this list is

QuietStaff Ltd (Co. No. 17171308) acts as a Data Processor under UK GDPR for our Customers' personal data. To deliver the Services, we engage the Sub-processors listed below. Each Sub-processor is bound by a written Data Processing Agreement that imposes data-protection obligations no less onerous than those we owe to our Customers.

This list operates in conjunction with our Privacy Policy and forms part of every signed QuietStaff Data Processing Agreement (DPA). Customer authorisation under the Sub-Processors clause of the DPA covers every Sub-processor on this list as at the date of signature. We give Customers at least 30 days' prior written notice of the addition of any new Sub-processor.

What is NOT a Sub-processor

The following providers are used by QuietStaff for our own internal operations and do not process Customer Personal Data on Customer's behalf, and are therefore not Sub-processors under UK GDPR Article 28:

  • Banking providers (Tide, Wise) — process QuietStaff's own financial data only
  • Internal accounting / payroll providers — process QuietStaff staff data only

Change-notification process

For each material change to this list (addition or removal of a Sub-processor):

  1. The change is staged at least 30 days before going live
  2. All current Customers are notified by email to the address registered on their signed DPA
  3. The change appears on this page with a (NEW — added YYYY-MM-DD) marker for 60 days
  4. Customers retain the right to object under the Sub-Processors clause of their signed DPA

Snapshot retention

For every signed Customer DPA, QuietStaff retains a snapshot of this page as it existed at the date of execution. This snapshot is available to the relevant Customer on request as evidence of their authorisation.
